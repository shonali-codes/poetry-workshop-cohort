The Power of Poetry

A six-week journey through ten modules. Four poets, one facilitator, a weekend rhythm, and the quiet craft of reading and writing with reverence.

Our Shared Ground

How we will walk these six weeks together

I am not your teacher. I am a co-poet, a co-learner. At certain moments I will wear the cape of facilitator — to hold structure, to protect our time, to guide the flow. Otherwise we are together in this as fellow poets.

Four things to carry with you through every module:

Structure
There is a shape to this workshop. Ten modules, across six weeks, each with its own intention and arc. All of us will try to honour it together. The structure is what makes the depth possible.
Safety
If at any point anything feels uncomfortable, tell me. We will look at it together. Your signals matter here. Nothing about this workshop is designed to be endured — it is designed to be entered, at whatever depth feels right for you.
Transformation
Sometimes, when we look at discomfort together, we will find it is the doorway — the place where the transformation is waiting. Some of the deepest shifts in a poet's life come through exactly the moments that felt hard at first. We will not run from those moments. We will meet them together.
Exploration in a safe space
Together we will discern which discomfort is a doorway and which is asking to be honoured as a limit. This discernment is a shared practice, not a rule. You are not alone in it.
I am with you.
Explore yourself in a safe space.
